Roadmap

Building Notachain

The order is fixed. The speed depends on us.

Phase 0 — Now

Foundation

The thesis, the documents, and the community.

Whitepaper published
Economics paper published
Jurisdiction registry published
Website live
Community channels open
Token name and ticker defined ($NTC)
Goal Validate demand. Build community. Find early believers and collaborators.
Phase 1

Token Launch

The economic foundation. No protocol functionality yet — this is what everything else builds on.

$NTC ERC-20 token contract deployed
Initial liquidity pool created
Treasury contracts deployed
Standard authorisation process live
Quarterly spending cap enforced
Team vesting schedule initiated
Goal Establish the token, the treasury, and the governance primitives.
Phase 2

Registry & Staking

The first protocol contracts. Attestors can register and stake.

Registry contract deployed
Staking contract with progressive vesting
Attestor grant distribution begins
Protocol entity operational for verification and key rotation
Out-of-band verification guidance published
Goal First attestors on-chain. Real professionals with real credentials and real stake.
Phase 3

Attestation Layer

The core primitive goes live. Booleans can be created and stored.

Attestation contract deployed (request-response)
IPFS integration for proof documents
Attestation signing tool for non-technical professionals
First attestations signed and published
Goal End-to-end protocol. A wallet holder gets a fact attested and any protocol can verify it.
Phase 4

Read-Fee Gate

Attestations become revenue-generating assets.

Read-fee contract deployed
Three-way fee split operational
Escalating fee mechanism for profiling resistance
Burn mechanics live
Emission model activated (inverse sigmoid)
Goal The economic flywheel starts. Holders earn. Attestors earn. The protocol burns.
Phase 5

Dispute Resolution

Accountability gets teeth beyond legal liability.

Dispute resolution contract deployed
Bond mechanism for challenging attestations
Cross-jurisdictional panel selection
Slashing execution (full/partial, burn/redistribute)
Goal Fraudulent attestations have immediate on-chain consequences.
Phase 6

DAO Governance

Progressive decentralisation begins.

DAO structure deployed
Institutional grant distribution begins
Referral reward mechanism live
DAO governs reference list, parameters, and treasury
Protocol entity authority narrows to operational and emergency
Goal Governed by institutional participants. Protocol entity becomes actor of last resort.
Phase 7

Ecosystem Growth

The primitive is consumed by other protocols.

First insurance product on Notachain attestations
Lending protocol integrations
Identity and credentialing integrations
Privacy enhancement layer (potential ZK integration)
Multi-chain deployment
Goal Notachain attestations become a standard trust primitive across the on-chain ecosystem.

This roadmap is deliberately undated. Phases are sequential — each depends on the one before it — but the pace depends on community growth, funding, and technical capacity. What is fixed: the order. What is flexible: the speed.
